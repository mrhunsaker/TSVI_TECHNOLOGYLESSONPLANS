Section 2.28 Change Fonts

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Open the "font" menu.

  • Turn bold on or off when necessary.

  • Turn italics on or off.

  • Underline text when desired.

  • Explore fonts to determine what is best for your situation.

Subsection 2.28.1 Tasks

  1. Create a new document.

  2. Change the font size.

  3. Press enter with f. You will read the question "font?"

  4. At this point you can enter a letter of the alphabet. For example, you may enter the letter h if you want to turn "20 characters per inch" on or off.

  5. To turn an option on, press n. Press f to turn an option off. Press enter to leave the item unchanged. Fonts may change according to which printer you are using.   6. If you turn bold on by using enter with f, b, then n for on, you can write your text that you want to be in bold font. If you never turn this off, the entire document will be in bold text. Turn the font off when you no longer want text to be in bold. To turn bold off, press enter with f, b, then the letter f for off.

  7. If you are unable to read printed text and you need to use a variety of font styles, ask someone to assist you with exploring different font types.

    1. Explore to find what is best for you.
