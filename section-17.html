Section 1.17 Extra Practice 3: Word Processing III

Put the following information in an accessible format for your student. They may follow the instructions to complete the assignment. Observe the student and give help as necessary or assign the work for independent practice at home. Check work and give feedback to ensure understanding. Adapt the assignment as you need to.

Subsection 1.17.1 Task

  1. Begin at main menu.

  2. Open the folder titled "Braille."

  3. In the Braille folder, create a file called "Practice 3."

  4. Write your name at the top of the page.

  5. Go to the next line.

  6. Write or insert the date.

  7. Go to the next line

  8. Write Practice 3.

  9. Go down 2 lines

  10. Write each question.

    1. Do you have knowledge about that?

    2. Will you play the game again?

    3. Is your dog cute?

    4. Would you help us?

    5. Will it get very hot?

  11. Read and edit your sentences for errors. Make any corrections that you find.

  12. Exit the document to the main menu
