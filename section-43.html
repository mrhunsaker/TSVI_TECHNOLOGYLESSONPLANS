Section 2.20 Set up Bluetooth

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Set up Bluetooth for embossing or printing

  • Print a document with Bluetooth

  • Emboss a document through Bluetooth

Subsection 2.20.1 Tasks

  1. Plug your Bluetooth adapter into the printer or the embosser. Plug the electric end into the wall outlet.

  2. Now match the Bluetooth adapter with the embosser or the printer. Press

  3. with space to go to the options menu. Press c for connect.

  4. Press b for Bluetooth.

  5. "Bluetooth on" will be your cue. Press y for yes.

  6. "Search for device?" is your next prompt. Press y for yes.

  7. The display will read, "Searching for device, please wait."

  8. You will get a list of Bluetooth devices that the BrailleNote found. Space to your device and press enter.

  9. You will then be given a list of services for the device. Space to the service you want and press enter. For example, space to "printer" and press enter.

  10. You will be asked if you want to pair with the device and activate the printer. Press y to pair with and activate the printer.

  11. "Authentication code" is the next cue. Press enter.

  12. You should receive a message saying that the BrailleNote is paired with the printer or embosser.

  13. Return to the Main Menu.

  14. Press w to open the keyword menu.

  15. Press p for print or e for emboss.

  16. Press s to setup the embosser or printer.

  17. Space to the "printer port" option. Press space with dots3 4 to toggle among the choices. The port you want is Bluetooth. Press e with space to exit when you are finished.

  18. When asked to confirm the changes, press y for yes.

  19. Now print or emboss the document.

  20. From the Keyword menu, press p for print or e for emboss.

  21. Answer with y when you are ready to print or to emboss.
