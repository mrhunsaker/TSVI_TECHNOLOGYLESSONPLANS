Section 2.16 Games

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Play a game on the BrailleNote.

  • Get help within games as needed

Subsection 2.16.1 Tasks

  1. To get to the Games Menu, space to games or press g from Main Menu. You may also press backspace with enter with g from anywhere on the BrailleNote to open the games.

  2. "Play which game?" will be displayed when the Games option is open. Computer Braille is required.

  3. Space to the tutorial game. Press enter.

  4. Use the thumb-keys to read with the Braille display. This will be a good way for older students to practice Braille reading skills in a fun situation.

  5. Read the instructions. Type in "about" to learn more about the game. Use computer Braille. Press enter.

  6. Read about the game.

  7. If you have a student who needs voice, turn on the voice with the previous thumb-key and space. The speech will help you learn the game during the tutorial session.

  8. Some game commands are listed below. You may want to read them all through the Games Help Menu to see if there are others you want to use.

    1. Type a single character to indicate your next move.

    2. Backspace with s to review the current status.

    3. Space with 5 6 and space with 2 3 will move through your move history list.

    4. Press space with s to save the current game.

    5. Press enter with o to open a game that you previously saved.

    6. Backspace with r will insert the last entered move.

    7. To move back by sentence, press space with dot1. Press space with dots1 4 to read the current sentence. Press space with dot4 to move forward by sentence.

    8. Backspace with q will abandon the current game.

  9. Here are some commands you will use during games.

    1. Use l to look around.

    2. Use i to obtain an inventory and you will get a closer look.

    3. The letter n is for north.

    4. s south

    5. w west

    6. e east

  10. Explore using the commands to play the game. Use space with h to get help when necessary. Try other games as time allows.
