Section 1.21 Miscellaneous: View BrailleNote on Monitor via VGA

  1. Connect BrailleNote to Monitor with VGA cable

  2. Open Options Menu space + o

  3. Open Visual Display letter v

  4. Turn on visual display letter v, enter

  5. You should see what's happening on the BrailleNote on the computer monitor.
