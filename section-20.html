Section 1.20 Miscellaneous: Use the Pronunciation Dictionary

Pronunciation Dictionary

  1. From Main Menu, open the Utilities Menu letter u

  2. Locate the Pronunciation Dictionary and open it

  3. The menu items

    • add a word

    • change a word

    • delete a world

  4. To add a word, focus on Add a Word and press enter.

  5. Type the word to add. Spell the word correctly. Use computer braille.

  6. Type the misspelling of the word so that it will be pronounced correctly.

  7. Press enter.

  8. The word has been added.

  9. Use the other menu options to change or delete a word.
