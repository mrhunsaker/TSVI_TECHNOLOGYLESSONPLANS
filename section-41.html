Section 2.18 Printing

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Print a document

Subsection 2.18.1 Tasks

  1. From Main Menu, press w to open the keyword menu.

  2. To print a document, press p for print.

  3. Press s for printer settings.

  4. Space to move through the list of printer setup options.

  5. Change settings as necessary. However, most settings should be fine as they are.

  6. The printer port may need to be changed. The port choices are bluetooth, infra-red, serial, USB, or file. Press space with dots3 4 to toggle to the printer port you are using.

  7. Space to continue through the list of options. You may need to change the printer type. If you do, press y for "yes" on this option. Then press enter.

  8. Use the thumb-keys to space to the type of printer you have. Press enter.

  9. Press e with space when you are finished changing the settings. When asked to confirm the changes, type y for yes.

  10. Now from the keyword options menu, press p for print. When asked if you want to print or setup the printer, press p for print.

  11. Enter on the folder and then the file that you want to print.

  12. Respond with y when asked if the printer is ready.

  13. Hopefully your document will print.

Subsection 2.18.2 Quiz

True or False:

  1. Bluetooth is an option for printing.

  2. The type of printer cannot be changed.

  3. Enter with o will take you to the options menu.

  4. Space with c will clear the calculator.

  5. You can print from within the keyword menu.

  6. Press b from Main Menu to get to the book reader.

  7. Enter with dot1 will make the voice volume softer.

  8. It is not possible to use the internet with the BrailleNote.

  9. The back and advance thumb-keys will move back and forward by line.

  10. Use backspace to delete the last character that was written.
