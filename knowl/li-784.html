Item 1.

You can open the dictionary/thesaurus from anywhere on the BrailleNote.

  1. Open Options Menu

  2. Select "Look up" or press l

  3. "Use dictionary or thesaurus?" is the cue.

  4. Press d for dictionary.

  5. "Word to look up in dictionary?" is the cue.

  6. Write the word you want to look up.

  7. Press enter.

  8. You can use the back or advance thumb-key to read the braille.
