Item 17.

When you are focused on or in an e-mail message, you can access the e-mail action menu by pressing space with dots2 and 6. "E-mail action menu" will be spoken or displayed. The choices in the e-mail action menu are:

  • Reply

  • Forward

  • Move e-mail to another folder

  • Copy this e-mail to another folder

  • Delete this e-mail from this folder

  • Print this e-mail

  • Emboss this e-mail

  • All. Mark all e-mail
