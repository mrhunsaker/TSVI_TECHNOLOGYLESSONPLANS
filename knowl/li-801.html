Item 3.

Use dictionary from within a document:

  1. Open your document.

  2. Place the cursor on the word you want to look up.

  3. Press space + o.

  4. Press letter l for "look up".

  5. Press d for dictionary.

  6. Notice the word your cursor was on in the document is already displayed after the "word to look up" cue.

  7. Press enter to look up the word.

  8. Use dictionary commands to read.
