Item 8.

If a word cannot be found or you need to fix a word, use the spell checker to do so. The following options are available when trying to fix a misspelled word:

  1. Use i to ignore all occurrences of the word.

  2. Space with dot5 will fix only the occurrence of the word that your student is currently working with.

  3. The letter a will add the word to the spelling dictionary.

  4. The letter c will help correct the word.

  5. To look up a word, press l.

  6. For suggestions, press s.

  7. Practice checking words at this time until you and your student adjust to using the word check option.
