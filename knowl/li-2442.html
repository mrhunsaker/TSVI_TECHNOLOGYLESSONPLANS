Item.

Concave-shaped Power/lock button

  • To start the BrailleNote Touch, hold down the Power button for about two seconds. You will see a Starting message in Braille, then a dot moving in a circular motion.

  • When the machine boots up, press space+U to unlock the screen.

  • You can lock or unlock the screen of the BrailleNote Touch by tapping the Power/lock button.
