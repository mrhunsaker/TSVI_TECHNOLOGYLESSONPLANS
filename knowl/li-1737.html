Item 8.

Some game commands are listed below. You may want to read them all through the Games Help Menu to see if there are others you want to use.

  1. Type a single character to indicate your next move.

  2. Backspace with s to review the current status.

  3. Space with 5 6 and space with 2 3 will move through your move history list.

  4. Press space with s to save the current game.

  5. Press enter with o to open a game that you previously saved.

  6. Backspace with r will insert the last entered move.

  7. To move back by sentence, press space with dot1. Press space with dots1 4 to read the current sentence. Press space with dot4 to move forward by sentence.

  8. Backspace with q will abandon the current game.
