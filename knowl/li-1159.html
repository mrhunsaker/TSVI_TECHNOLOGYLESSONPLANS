Item 23.

In the message area, write a simple e-mail message. Edit the message as you wish before sending it. A review of some editing commands is given below.

  1. Delete to end of sentence by using backspace with dots1 and 4.

  2. To delete the word under the cursor, use backspace with dots2 and 5.

  3. Use backspace with dot2 to delete the word before the cursor.

  4. To delete to the end of the paragraph, press backspace with dots2 3 5 6.

  5. Use dots4 5 6 with backspace to delete from the cursor to the end of the message.

  6. Use the cursor keys and backspace to make corrections and insertions.
