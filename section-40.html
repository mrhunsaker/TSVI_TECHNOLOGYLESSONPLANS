Section 2.19 Embossing

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Emboss a document

Subsection 2.19.1 Tasks

  1. From Main Menu, press w to open the keyword menu.

  2. To emboss a document, press e for emboss.

  3. The first time you emboss, press s for embosser settings.

  4. Space to move through the list of embosser setup options.

  5. Change settings as necessary. However, most settings should be fine as they are.

  6. The embosser port may need to be changed. The port choices are:

    1. Bluetooth

    2. Infra-red

    3. Serial

    4. USB

    5. File

  7. Press space with dots3 4 to toggle to the embosser port you are using.

  8. Space to continue through the list of options. You may need to change the embosser type. If you do, press y for "yes" on this option. Then press enter.

  9. Use the thumb-keys to move to the type of embosser you have. Press enter. Press e with space when you are finished changing the settings. When asked to confirm the changes, type y for yes.

  10. Now from the keyword options menu, press e for emboss. When asked if you want to emboss or setup the embosser, press e for emboss.

  11. Enter on the folder and then file that you want to emboss.

  12. Respond with y when asked if the embosser is ready.

  13. Hopefully your document will emboss.

Subsection 2.19.2 Extra Commands

  1. Enter with d will speak the date.

  2. Enter with t will speak the time.

  3. Backspace of dots4 5 6 will delete to the end of the file.

  4. Use backspace with 1 4 to delete to the end of the current sentence.

  5. To open a program from anywhere, press backspace with enter with the initial letter of the program. For example, to open the planner press enter with backspace + p

  6. Space with a full cell will return to the main menu.
