Section 2.17 Dictionary

Skills Addressed in this Lesson:

  • Look up a word in the dictionary.

  • Read through a list of suggested words.

  • Read through a list of word definitions.

  • Insert a word from the dictionary into a document.

Subsection 2.17.1 Tasks

This lesson assumes that you have the Concise Oxford Dictionary loaded onto your BrailleNote.

  1. In a document, write a word you would like to look up in the dictionary.

  2. Place the cursor somewhere on the word.

  3. Press o with space, then l. L is for look up word.

  4. To look up a word in the dictionary, press d.

  5. Press enter to look up the word that is displayed.

  6. To move forward through the list of suggestions, press space. Backspace will move back through the suggestions list. Press e with space to exit the list.

  7. If you find the word you want to look up, press enter on the word.

  8. Space with i will give you information about the word you are looking up.

  9. Enter with dots2 5 will pronounce the word you are looking up.

  10. Space with 2 3 will move back to the previous entry. Space with dots5 6 will move to the next entry.

  11. If you want to insert the word you are looking up under the cursor, press backspace with i.

  12. Follow the above steps in order to explore looking up a word in the dictionary. Get help when necessary with space with h.

  13. From within a document, press space with o, then l to look up a word.

  14. Type in a word you want to look up. If you are unsure of a letter in the word, press a question mark with a space. This is dots2 3 6 with space.

  15. Press enter to look up the word. Use the commands previously listed to look up the word.

  16. Use backspace with k to copy a selection to the clipboard.

  17. Practice looking up words in the dictionary.

Subsection 2.17.2 Quiz

Answer each question with one of the two provided choices.

  1. What should you press to move forward through a list of suggestions?

  2. What with i will give you information about the word you are looking up?

  3. Space with what will move back to the previous entry?

  4. Backspace with what letter will copy an entry to the clipboard?

  5. What with dots2 3 will move back to the previous entry in the list?

  6. If you find a word you want to look up, which key should you press to look up the word?

  7. Enter with which dotswill pronounce the word you are looking up?

  8. If you are unsure of a letter in the word you are looking up, what can you press with space in place of the letter you do not know?

  9. What will pressing backspace with i do to the word you are looking up?

  10. Is it possible to read a full definition in the Concise Oxford Dictionary?
